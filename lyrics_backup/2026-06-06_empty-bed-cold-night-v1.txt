[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The fire is dying down to just a spark,
I'm reaching for your shadow in the dark.
This empty bed, this cold and quiet night,
Just need your body here to make it right.

[Verse 1]
I poured two glasses, left one by the door,
Your favorite jacket's lying on the floor.
I hear the wind come rattling through the pine,
Just wishing I could feel your skin on mine.

[Pre-Chorus]
And every minute feels a little longer,
This midnight craving's getting so much stronger.
I close my eyes and catch your sweet perfume,
I need your magic in this lonely room.

[Chorus]
Oh, this empty bed and this cold night,
I want to love you till the morning light.
I feel the distance tearing at my soul,
Come wrap me up and make my heartbeat whole.

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, come on over, baby, pull me close,
You are the only one I want the most.

[Verse 2]
I left the porch light shining through the rain,
Just hoping you would drive this way again.
I trace the pillow where you used to lay,
I'm counting down the hours till the day.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
And every minute feels a little longer,
This midnight craving's getting so much stronger.
I close my eyes and catch your sweet perfume,
I need your magic in this lonely room.

[Chorus]
Oh, this empty bed and this cold night,
I want to love you till the morning light.
I feel the distance tearing at my soul,
Come wrap me up and make my heartbeat whole.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, come on over, baby, pull me close,
You are the only one I want the most.

[Bridge]
The silence whispers secrets in my ear,
I only want to feel your body near.
Just tell me what you're waiting for right now,
I want to kiss the worry from your brow.
Come give your heart into the candlelight,
Saeed will love you through this lonely night.

[Spoken]
Don't keep me waiting in the dark anymore,
Just turn the handle, walk right through that door.

[Final Chorus]
Oh, this empty bed and this cold night,
I want to love you till the morning light.
I feel the distance tearing at my soul,
Come wrap me up and make my heartbeat whole.
